## The Training Problem

Training a large neural network from scratch requires:
- **Enormous datasets**: GPT-3 was trained on ~500 billion tokens of text
- **Massive compute**: weeks to months on thousands of GPUs
- **Huge cost**: GPT-3's training is estimated at $4–12 million USD

For almost every practical application, training from scratch is not feasible. Pre-trained models solve this by front-loading the expensive general learning so you don't have to.
