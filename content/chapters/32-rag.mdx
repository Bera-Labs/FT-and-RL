## What Problem Does RAG Solve?

Fine-tuning bakes knowledge into model weights — permanent, fast at inference, but costly to update and limited to what was in training data. RAG externalizes knowledge into a retrievable database — always up-to-date, can be arbitrarily large, but adds retrieval latency.

## How RAG and Fine-Tuning Complement Each Other

RAG and fine-tuning aren't mutually exclusive — they solve different problems and can be layered.
