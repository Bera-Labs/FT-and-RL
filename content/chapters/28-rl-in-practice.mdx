<ConceptCheck
  question="Your PPO agent trains on a game for 500,000 steps. Reward rises steadily to +120 by step 200,000, then suddenly drops to +30 and oscillates around there for the remaining 300,000 steps. What are the two most likely causes, and what would you check first?"
  answer="The two most likely causes are: (1) Policy instability — a batch of data caused an update that pushed the policy too far, collapsing previously-learned behavior. Check: the KL divergence between old and new policy at the point of the drop. If KL > 0.1, reduce clip_range or learning rate. (2) Reward hacking reversal — the agent found a 'shortcut' that stopped working (e.g., environment was non-stationary or a glitch was patched). Check: log the actual agent behavior at both the peak and after the drop. I'd check the KL divergence first because instability is more common and easier to diagnose — sudden loss of policy is usually visible in the metrics log."
