## RL vs. Supervised Learning: When to Use Which

<MermaidDiagram chart={`graph TD
  classDef sup fill:#dbeafe,stroke:#60a5fa,color:#1e3a8a
  classDef rl fill:#dcfce7,stroke:#4ade80,color:#14532d

  USE_SL["Use Supervised Learning when:\n──────────────────────────────\n✓ You have labeled examples of correct behavior\n✓ The correct output is well-defined\n✓ You can collect training data in advance\nExamples: image classification, spam detection,\ntranslation, summarization"]
  USE_RL["Use Reinforcement Learning when:\n──────────────────────────────\n✓ Correct actions aren't known in advance\n✓ Feedback comes from the environment, not labels\n✓ Long sequences of decisions matter\n✓ You can simulate the environment\nExamples: games, robotics, RLHF alignment,\ntrading systems, recommendation engines"]
